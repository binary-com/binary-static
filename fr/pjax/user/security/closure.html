Before you deactivate your account, you need to do the following:

1. Close all open positions

If you have a DMT5 real account, log into it to close any open positions.

2. Withdraw your funds

If you have a Binary real account, go to Cashier to withdraw your funds

If you have a DMT5 real account, go to MT5 dashboard to withdraw your funds.
