1. Proof of identity

We accept:
  • Passport
  • Driving licence
  • National ID card, ID book or any government-issued document which contains a photo, your name, and date of birth
Requirements:
  • Must be a clear, colour photo or scanned image
  • Minimum of six months validity
  • Only JPG, JPEG, GIF, PNG and PDF formats are accepted
