Binary.com的統計數字

Binary.com自 2000 年以來開始營業， 是全球領先的二元期權公司。

活躍的交易客戶

Our active client base is expected to reach over 790,000 total clients this year.

交易次數

We're projected to register over 1.1 billion transactions this year, bringing the total number of contracts that were bought and sold on our platform since inception to over 2.8 billion.

雇員和承包商數目

We've grown in size over the years - both in terms of manpower and offices. We currently have over 440 employees across 10 offices in Malta, Dubai, Malaysia, Paraguay, Cyprus, Rwanda, and Belarus. Further growth in headcount is expected this year to cater to the increased demand in our products and services.
