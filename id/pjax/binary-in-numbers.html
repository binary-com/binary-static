Binary.com dalam Angka

Dalam bisnis sejak tahun 2000, Binary.com adalah perusahaan opsi binary pilihan terdepan dunia.

Klien trading aktif

Our active client base is expected to reach over 790,000 total clients this year.

Jumlah transaksi

We're projected to register over 1.1 billion transactions this year, bringing the total number of contracts that were bought and sold on our platform since inception to over 2.8 billion.

Jumlah karyawan dan kontraktor

We've grown in size over the years - both in terms of manpower and offices. We currently have over 500 employees across 10 offices in Malta, Dubai, Malaysia, Paraguay, Cyprus, Rwanda, and Belarus. Further growth in headcount is expected this year to cater to the increased demand in our products and services.
